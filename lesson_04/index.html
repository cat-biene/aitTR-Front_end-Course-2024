Bike

Bicycles, often referred to as bikes, are versatile and efficient modes of transportation that have stood the test of time. Dating back to the 19th century, these two-wheeled wonders have evolved from simple contraptions to sleek, high-tech machines, catering to a wide range of enthusiasts, from casual riders to competitive cyclists.

How does cycling affect your health

Health is a multifaceted concept encompassing physical, mental, and social well-being. Achieving and maintaining good health is a dynamic and ongoing process that involves a combination of lifestyle choices, environmental factors, and genetic predispositions.

What kind of bicycles are there

  • Road Bikes

    Designed for speed and efficiency on paved roads. Lightweight frames, drop handlebars, and narrow, high-pressure tires. Ideal for long-distance rides, racing, and fitness cycling.

  • Mountain Bikes

    Built for off-road trails and rugged terrain. Robust frames, wide, knobby tires, and suspension systems. Available in various subtypes like cross-country, trail, and downhill bikes.

  • Hybrid Bikes

    Blend features of road and mountain bikes. Versatile and suitable for a mix of on- and off-road riding. Comfortable riding position and medium-width tires.

  • Touring Bikes

    Built for long-distance travel and loaded with gear. Durable frames, multiple mounting points for racks and panniers. Comfortable geometry for extended periods of riding

  • Cruiser Bikes

    Known for their laid-back design and comfortable riding position. Wide, balloon tires, and often a single-speed drivetrain. Ideal for casual rides along the beach or around town.
